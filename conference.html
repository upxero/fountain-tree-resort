Conference Room

Fountain Tree Resort Khao Yai offers a variety of venues for events and meetings. Our air-conditioned conference rooms are fully equipped and can accommodate 30 to 300 guests. The open-air multi-purpose building is ideal for sports events and team-building activities, hosting up to 700 guests. The semi-circular garden amphitheater by the lake is perfect for performances and group events, with space for 600 guests. We also offer three outdoor banquet areas for themed events, accommodating 30 to 600 guests. Our facilities can cater to gatherings from 30 to 1,000 guests.
