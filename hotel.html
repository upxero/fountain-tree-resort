Cowboy Style or Private Room

There are various types of accommodations for you to choose from, including superior rooms and cowboy-style rooms, accommodating 2-4 people per room. Additionally, there are 10 private villas available. Moreover, there are 10 shelter-style accommodations that can house 10-80 people.

Private Room

For those seeking tranquility and privacy, our private villas offer the perfect retreat. Each villa provides a serene environment, ideal for couples or small groups looking to enjoy a peaceful stay. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway or a quiet family vacation, our private rooms promise comfort and exclusivity amidst the beauty of nature.
